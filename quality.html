We believe in QUALITY and deliver the same!




Our Quality Management Systems are in accordance with and are certified for ISO 9001: 2008.
We, at APOORV ELECTRICALS are committed to achieve technical excellence in Design, Manufacture and Services while maintaining high quality and timely delivery.

We are committed to producing quality products. All the raw materials are procured from carefully selected vendors and subjected to rigorous incoming inspection and quality checks before being used. We have adequate facility to ensure testing of raw materials like core, copper, transformer oil etc.

Furthermore, various in-process tests are conducted as per the quality plan during the manufacturing process. We have a dedicated team of engineers to conduct quality checks at various critical points of manufacturing. Accuracy of CT is checked at various stages. After tanking, the tanks are hermetically sealed and filtered and heated oil is filled under vacuum to prevent entrance of moisture.

Finally, each and every unit undergoes all the routine tests as per relevant IS/IEC standard and as per customer's requirements. We have a fully equipped testing laboratory where all the required instruments are calibrated regularly from NABL approved laboratory.
